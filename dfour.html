We can't say that our Life will always be a clear path because sometimes life throws obstacles in our path but it depends totally on us whether we stop there or try to find a new path.

My school phase went on very smoothly. Coming to my intermediate, in the first year I got 465 out of 470 in state exam and I was on cloud 9. I worked hard the same way in my second year but unfortunately I just got 954 out of 1000 in second year and I spoke not a word with my friends and teachers. I somehow managed to go and tell my parents, I reached home and stood infront of my mom as I spoke about my marks I tried to laugh but eventually I knew it wouldn't last. At that point i broke out i cried alot that even my parents got frightened. The main problem was I was very stressed as my JEE exams was right around the corner. With 2 days left to it my self esteem plunged down and I was getting panic attacks. I knew that this might have effected my jee exam. My parents helped me out and I did get introduced to mindfulness and meditation. The situation passed and Now I am persuaing B.Tech in Nit Patna in Ece department.

At that situation my parents backed me that they have belief in me and told me that i can achieve more..My teachers and friends encouraged me in the same way

So as i said at the start, Life throws some obstacles in our path , it doesn't mean it's a dead end... It is just a challenge whether we are dare enough to choose another path or make our own way...
