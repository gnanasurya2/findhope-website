Suicides are on a rise and Not many Campuses have in house professionals. But our Heros are here to save the day. Our youth Advocates use their struggles as tools to help fellow students.

What we look for in a Youth Advocate

  • Passionate about Mental Health.
  • Aware of symptoms and coping strategies of common mental health struggles.
  • Compassion for fellow students' struggle.
  • Aids peers to open up about their struggles.
