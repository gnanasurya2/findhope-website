And I couldn't play my part because I was unaware of the Idea of mental health.Before that, I have never felt what loneliness is and I started self analysing myself, which again depends on what do you know, what is your story, how much you know about other's stories. Through that self analysis I realized that I was happy earlier because I was expressing myself through art and writing and I was expressing only my happiness.I cared of what people will think if I start expressing my sorrows and gloom because . I never presented my self as an emotional being, but that one death changed it for me. Made me realize how important it is to express. No matter how good or bad you are at singing, writing, painting, talking. No matter how big you are at it. No matter what your audience size is. You can express it to one person you can express it to a crowd of thousands. But, we are a vessel of feelings and the vessel needs to be emptied again and again to make room for more. And yes you have the freedom to think that no one will resonate with your thoughts, you are free to feel feared of rejection, but at the same time you are free to be angry and you are free to express.
