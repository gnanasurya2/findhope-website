I always wanted to be a forensic expert so I chose to study BSc Forensic but life had another path in store,my parents thought it will be better off with a BSC Human physiology. The transition was hard, one side I had a yearning heart and another side, my parents, both were important, this took a toll on me and I went through a mental breakdown when I had to sacrifice my dream course and settle for something less.

I did talk with my parents, we sometimes had arguments but I could understand the financial pressures. I made my mind and joined a University as a student of Bsc Physiology, It was not yet over, I was advised to attend another University and I did it only to regret later. The new University had a very bad community so it started affecting me mentally and physically. It was a fix, I was stuck and didn't know what to do so, I took some time and talked to those very few whom I could lean on. It's true that I had no chance to pursue what I wanted so, I chose the best of what I had at my expose and returned to the previous University. I could have pushed my parents to pay the expensive Course I wanted but It would be of no use.

Some times we settle not Because we want to but because of its right. I am definitely going to chase my dream again after bachelor's its just this time gap between us.

Don't give up, sometimes we have to settle for less, it's fine and it's not the end only if we don't give up.
